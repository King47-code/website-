Coding for African Kids

Welcome to our coding school for kids aged 5 to 15! We believe that coding is an essential skill that every child should have in today's digital world. Our courses are designed to introduce coding concepts to young learners in a fun and interactive way, while also providing them with the necessary tools to become proficient coders.

Aims and Objectives

  • Introduce kids to coding concepts in a fun and interactive way
  • Build logical thinking and problem-solving skills in young learners
  • Develop proficiency in various programming languages such as Scratch, Python, Java, and JavaScript
  • Foster creativity and innovation through collaborative projects and challenges
  • Prepare kids for success in the digital world and the 21st century workforce

Mission Statement

Our mission at the coding school for kids is to provide a supportive and nurturing learning environment

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of coding courses for kids aged 5 to 15. Our courses are designed to introduce coding concepts to young learners in a fun and interactive way, while also providing them with the necessary tools to become proficient coders. Some of our popular courses include:

  • Introduction to Scratch
  • Python for Kids
  • Javascript and Web Development
  • Minecraft Modding
  • Robotics and Engineering
